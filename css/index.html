General guidelines
  • Your design should always be flowable.
  • Don't use absolute positioning too much. I would say avoid them as all cost, but there are some legitmate cases
  • Avoid explicitness on non-layout elements. Rather let the children determine their size
  • Use rem/em rather then pixels for padding, margins and font size. Let your page be in relation to your font size.
